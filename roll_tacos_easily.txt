This recipe works with watherver ingredients. Use your favourite recipe (e.g. cauliflour 
tacos) and follow these steps:
1. generously add fillings on top of the tortilla
2. fold teh tortilla and squish the filling
3. spread it a little bit
4. fold the bit closest to you over
5. fold in the sides
6 and roll the taco, applying generous pressure. do not tear the taco!
7. enjoy1
